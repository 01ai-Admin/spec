# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This is a technical project. Participation is expected to be:

- **Substantive** — contributions should be grounded in the spec and evidence
- **Respectful** — disagree with ideas, not people
- **Direct** — say what you mean clearly and concisely
- **Good faith** — assume other contributors are trying to improve the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews
- Bad-faith contributions intended to damage the project

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards, and may ban participants who repeatedly violate them.

Report issues to: **info@01ai.ai**
